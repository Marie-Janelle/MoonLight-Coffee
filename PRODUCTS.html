Coffees

Espresso

A rich and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's the base for many other coffee drinks like cappuccino and latte.

Americano

An espresso shot diluted with hot water, creating a coffee thatâ€™s less intense than espresso but still full of flavor.

Latte

A creamy coffee made with espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a small amount of foam. It has a smooth, mellow flavor.

Cappuccino

A balanced drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. Cappuccinos are slightly stronger than lattes, with a rich and bold taste.

Macchiato

An espresso "stained" with a small amount of foamed milk, resulting in a stronger, more intense coffee flavor.

Mocha

A latte with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder added, combining the sweetness of chocolate with the rich flavor of coffee. It's perfect for those with a sweet tooth.
